Projects

These projects are from my previous education as a product designer and also from current education.

Zimo | Portable sleepaid 

A good night's sleep is essential for a child's development.

Zimo is a portable aid for 0 to 5+ year olds. One the one hand it helps to de-stimulate the child during its first year of life by using a heartbeat sound and on the other hand reduces separation anxiety and night fears when they get older by using light. 

Kiko | Babyphone design 

A babyphone concept inspired by a certain persona. 

The goal of this assignment was to create a wearable through the use of CAD. In doing so, the techniques learned from class had to be applied in the final design. 

Mello | Oil Pepper Salt 

An assignment for the identity course. 

A three-piece set, consisting of a salt and pepper shaker and a small oil can. Accessible and clean design, with a touch of soft warmth. 

Identity | Coffee mug

Shape a coffee mug based on your identity as a designer. 

As a creative mind, I try to form products with an honest and a warm, rather mellow feeling. Accessible and clean design, with a certain finesse, with the intention to unite the product and the end-user.  

Stitch | Animated Robot

Create a robot for the course of Emerging Technologies. 

The goal of this assignment is to use javascript to create a game in which the robot takes on a particular enemy in a world.
