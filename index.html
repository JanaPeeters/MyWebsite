About me 

I am a 22 year old Industrial Engineering Design student at Ugent Campus Kortrijk.                                                              
I already have a bachelor's degree in Industrial Product Design at the University of Applied Sciences at Howest. Besides designing, I am also very interested in photography, and cooking. 

My vision 

As a product designer I make products with a balance between functionality and aesthetics. My strength lies in the research around the use of the product. Design for me is a way of communication. Making an idea tangible and visual, by transferring these ideas through sketches on paper, making prototypes, mockups and testing the product with the end user.
